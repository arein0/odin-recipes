Chili

Description

This recipe will help you make chili.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 15 ounces tomato sauce
  • 1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans, drained
  • 1 (15 ounce) can chili beans, not drained
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder, or to taste
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

    • Preparation

    1. Heat a large skillet over medium-heat. Cook and stir ground beef in the skillet until browned and crumbly (5-7 minutes)
    2. Stir in kidney beans, chili beans, and tomato sauce
    3. Bring to a boil and stir in chili powder
    4. Reduce heat to a simmer, and cook, stirring occasionally, until thickened, about 15 minutes
    5. Season to taste with salt and pepper
