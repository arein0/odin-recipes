Grilled Cheese

Description

This recipe will help you make a classic Grilled Cheese Sandwich.

Ingredients

  • Sliced Butter
  • Sliced Cheese
  • 2 slices of bread

    • Preparation

    1. Butter the bread on one side and place the bread butter-side down
    2. Top with cheese
    3. Place another slice of butter bread, butter-side up
    4. Cook until the bottom slice is lightly browned, then flip
    5. Continue cooking until the cheese is melted
